Goats are masters of sustainability.

Goats selectively graze for invasive weeds, helping increase biodiversity. Goats, when responsibly raised, can be part of a regenerative agriculture. Regenerative agriculture helps combat climate change by connecting humans with the land through any range of self-sustaining practices.

Their carbon footprint is lower than many livestock, their meat has a greater nutritional value, and their self-selection of deep rooted plants helps recycle trace minerals back to the surface!
